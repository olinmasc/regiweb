My Story
Your image speaks before you do. Regina Pinto has built her career around one powerful truth — that when you look the part, own the room, and communicate with confidence, everything changes.

As a Certified Image Consultant and Corporate Trainer, Regina helps professionals, entrepreneurs, and organisations transform the way they show up — and the results they get.

Regina holds a professional certification in Image Management from the Image Consulting Business Institute (ICBI), accredited by the globally recognised Conselle Institute of Image Management, USA. Her expertise is further backed by four prestigious certifications from the Indian Leadership Academy — Train the Trainer, Certified Corporate Trainer, Certified Soft Skills Trainer, and Certified Master Trainer — making her one of the most comprehensively qualified image and soft skills professionals in the country.

Her work goes far beyond styling. Regina's sessions are structured, practical, and built for real transformation — covering executive presence, personal branding, business etiquette, grooming, and communication skills.

Whether she's working with a C-suite leader, a fresh graduate stepping into corporate life, or an entire organisation — her approach remains the same: align your image with your ambition, and watch your career follow.
